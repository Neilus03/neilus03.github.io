## Research Experience

### Computer Vision Center (CVC)
**Duration:** August 2023 - Present  
**Role:** Research Student  
**Focus:** Computer Vision applied to Medical Image Understanding under the supervision of Dr. Jorge Bernal.

- Contributed to various projects' ideation, development, and delivery as the first author.
- Developed a system for the automatic assessment of cleanliness in esophagogastroduodenoscopy (EGD) images using a combination of synthetic and real data.
- Achieved significant quantitative improvements, including an 18% increase in accuracy for the most underrepresented class and a 6% overall improvement in global accuracy and precision.
