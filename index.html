About Me

Hi, I'm Neil. I'm an AI student and researcher from Barcelona, focusing on deep learning, computer vision, and NLP. I'm currently working on 4D generation at TUM under Prof. Daniel Cremers. I have also worked at the Computer Vision Center (CVC) with Dr. Jorge Bernal, and at HiTZ with Dr. Eneko Agirre. My interests revolve around self-supervised learning, video understanding, and generative modeling.
