About Me

Hi, I'm Neil. I'm an AI student and researcher from The Basque Country, focusing on deep learning, computer vision, and NLP. I'm currently working on 4D generation at TUM under Prof. Daniel Cremers. I have also worked at the CVC with Dr. Jorge Bernal, and at HiTZ with Dr. Eneko Agirre. My interests revolve around self-supervised learning, video understanding, 3D computer vision, and generative modeling.
