About Me

Hi, I'm Neil. I'm an AI student and researcher from Barcelona, focusing on deep learning, computer vision, and NLP. I have also worked at the Computer Vision Center (CVC) with Dr. Jorge Bernal, and at HiTZ with Dr. Eneko Agirre. My interests revolve around self-supervised learning, video understanding, and generative modeling.
